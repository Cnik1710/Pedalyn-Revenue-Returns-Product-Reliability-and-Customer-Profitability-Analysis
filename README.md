# Pedalyn - Revenue, Returns & Product Reliability and Customer Profitability Analysis
## 🎯 Objective
## **Project Purpose:**
To analyze retail performance across Returns, Revenue, and Profitability and uncover actionable business insights. And also, help stakeholders identify revenue drivers, minimize returns, and maximize profitability.
### **Key KPIs:**
   * Total Orders
   * Total Revenue
   * Total Profit
   * Return Rate (%)
   * Average Order Value (AOV)
   * Profit Margin (%)
   * Total Customers / Returned Products
### **Deliverables:**
   * Interactive Power BI Dashboards
   * Revenue Performance
   * Returns & Product Reliability
   * Customer Profitability
   * Business insights & recommendations
   * Data-driven decision support framework
---

## 📘 Project Overview 
### **Context Highlights:**
This project analyzes a multi-category retail business to evaluate performance gaps, risks, and profitability drivers. In a competitive retail environment, understanding what drives revenue and what causes losses is critical.
The business deals with product categories such as Bikes, Accessories, and Clothing, with revenue heavily concentrated in select segments.
Key challenges identified include:
   * High product return rates, impacting overall efficiency
   * Revenue concentration in limited categories, increasing business risk
   * Variations in customer profitability, indicating uneven value distribution
This analysis was designed to identify inefficiencies and uncover actionable, data-driven insights.
The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights and strategic business decisions using Power BI.

## 🗂️ Data Overview & Schema     
### **Data Source:**  
   * Source: Structured retail dataset (Sales, Products, Customers, Returns, Territories)
   * Data Type: Relational (Fact + Dimension tables)
   * Time Period: 2015 – 2017
### **Data Structure & Metrics:** 
   * Key Index Types: ProductKey, CustomerKey, TerritoryKey, OrderNumber
   * Categories: Bikes, Accessories, Clothing
   * Fact Table: sales_combined
   * Dimension Tables: customers, products, product_categories, territories, returns
   * Calculated Metrics:
      * Revenue = Quantity × Price
      * Profit = Quantity × (Price – Cost)
      * Return Rate = Returns / Sales
      * Profit Margin = Profit / Revenue
      * Income Groups (Low / Middle / High)

## ❓ Problem Statements
To address the business challenges and uncover meaningful insights, this analysis focuses on answering critical questions related to revenue generation, return risks, and customer behavior. The objective is to identify key drivers of performance, areas of inefficiency, and opportunities for growth across products, customers, and regions.

### Key Questions:
   *  Which products and categories drive maximum revenue and profit?
   * Which products have high return rates and why?
   * Are sales growing consistently over time?
   * Which customers contribute the most to revenue and profit?
   * How does customer behavior vary by income, gender, and geography?
   * Which regions and segments are underperforming?
---

## 💡 Key Insights      
### **Top Findings:** 
   * Bikes category dominates revenue (~5.2M) while others contribute minimally.
   * Return Rate is ~11.64%, indicating significant product quality or expectation issues.
   * Certain products (e.g., Road & Mountain series) show high return rates → risk area.
   * Revenue trend shows fluctuations, with peak growth in mid-2016.
   * Profit is highly concentrated in specific models (Mountain-200).
   * Middle-income customers contribute highest profit, showing strong purchasing power.

### **Supporting Metrics:**
   * Total Revenue: 5.42M
   * Total Profit: 2.35M
   * Profit Margin: ~43%
   * Total Returns: 1,828
   * Top Product Revenue: ~0.45M
   * Top Model Profit: ~1.16M
---
 
## 📍 Conclusion
### **Summary:** 
   * The business is revenue-strong but operationally inefficient due to returns.

   * Profitability is concentrated, indicating dependency on limited products.

   * Customer segmentation reveals high-value target groups.
   
   * Focus should be on:
     * Reducing returns
     * Diversifying revenue sources
     * Targeting high-profit customer segments

## ✅ Business Impact & Use Cases   
This analysis translates data insights into practical business actions that drive measurable impact across operations, marketing, and strategy. By leveraging data-driven findings, the business can reduce inefficiencies, enhance customer targeting, and improve overall profitability, enabling smarter and more informed decision-making.

  * Helps reduce return losses by identifying problem products. Pinpoints products with high return rates, enabling businesses to investigate root causes like quality issues, pricing mismatch, or customer expectations, and take corrective actions.

  * Enables targeted marketing for high-value customers. Identifies top-performing customer segments based on revenue and profitability, allowing for personalized campaigns, better engagement, and higher conversion rates.

  * Supports inventory & pricing decisions. Provides insights into high-demand and low-performing products, helping optimize stock levels, avoid overstocking, and implement competitive pricing strategies.

  * Assists leadership in data-driven strategic planning. Offers a clear view of business performance across products, regions, and customers, enabling leaders to make informed, strategic decisions backed by data.

  * Improves profitability through product & segment optimization. Highlights high-margin products and profitable segments, allowing the business to focus resources, refine offerings, and maximize overall profitability.
